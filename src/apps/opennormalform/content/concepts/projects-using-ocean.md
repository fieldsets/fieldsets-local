## OceanDAO projects

[Ocean Pearl](https://oceanpearl.io/projects) is a great way to browse 80+ Ocean projects that came through [OceanDAO](https://opennormalform.org/dao). These projects may be building on the Ocean stack, doing outreach, unlocking data, or more.

## Using Ocean Market

[Ocean Market](https://market.opennormalform.org) is the best place to find projects that publish datasets. Simply go there and browse the datasets:)

## Learning about Ocean

The [Ocean Academy](https://oceanacademy.io/) project is a great way to learn more about Ocean beyond [opennormalform.org](https://www.opennormalform.org) and [docs.opennormalform.org](https://docs.opennormalform.org).

## Trading OCEAN

The [Coingecko OCEAN markets page](https://www.coingecko.com/en/coins/ocean-protocol#markets) lists forums to exchange OCEAN. Many of them offer liquidity mining and other yield opportunities.
